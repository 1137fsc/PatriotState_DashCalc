Formula Reference

Electrical Power Input
Pin = √3 × V × I × PF / 1000

Where V = line voltage, I = current, PF = power factor

Hydraulic Power Output
HPhyd = (Q × H × SG) / 3960

Where Q = GPM, H = head (ft), SG = specific gravity

Wire-to-Water Efficiency
ηW2W = HPhyd / HPelec × 100%

Captures VFD, motor, and pump losses

Affinity Law - Power
P2 = P1 × (N2/N1)³

Power scales with speed CUBED
